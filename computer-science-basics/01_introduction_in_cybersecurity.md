## 🧠 Core Concepts Learned   

### Offensive Security:  
- Ethical hacking in controlled environment  
- Simulating attacks to understand vulnerabilities  
- Importance of legality and responsible disclosure  

### Defensive Security:  
- Protecting systems from attacks  
- Includes Threat Intelligence, SOC operations, DFIR, Malware Analysis, and SIEM tools  
- Monitoring, detection, and incident response  

### Cybersecurity Careers:  
- Roles include SOC Analyst, Penetration Tester, Malware Analyst, Threat Intel Analyst  
- Each role requires a mix of technical skills and problem-solving
